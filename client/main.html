WELCOME TO VAXIME

Vaxime is a self-diagnosis tool meant for tracking and advising on possible side effects that appear due to the COVID vaccine.
By employing machine learning (ML), the data inputted to the system is analyzed and congregated to create a personalized report to tell you what possible side effects you may experience.
The data you input will then be stored and help to create more accurate predictions in the future
